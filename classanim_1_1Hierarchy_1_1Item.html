Member Data Documentation

Attributes anim::Hierarchy::Item::attrs
private

Private attributes - item instances and Hierarchy iterators are only exposed as const (non-const operations are fully managed by the Hierarchy class, to guarantee consistency). However, the attributes have to be accessible on a non-const Hierarchy instance as non-const, without allowing the user to change parents, children or other things on an Item. For that reason, they are exposed via itemAttributes() instead of directly on Item.
